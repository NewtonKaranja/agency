About Us

About Us

In Kenya's fast-evolving economy, SMEs face real challenges: juggling multiple freelancers for ads and tech, navigating complex KRA compliance (eTIMS, SEP tax, iTax filings), and turning data into actionable growth amid tight credit and rising costs.

Elite Agency: Built for Kenyan SMEs

Nairobi-based EliteEdge combines digital marketing, custom web apps, data analysis, brand scaling, and KRA tax compliance to help fintech, e-commerce, and real estate SMEs achieve 2–5x growth—without fragmented freelancers or compliance headaches.

In 2026, Kenyan SMEs are going digital fast (91% use digital payments), but eTIMS real-time validation, SEP tax rules, and undocumented expense issues still drain cashflow. We deliver integrated, Nairobi-tailored solutions that drive measurable results from Westlands to the CBD.

  • Deep Nairobi expertise – We understand Kenyan consumer behavior, M-Pesa ecosystems, TikTok/Instagram trends, and KRA's evolving rules (eTIMS enforcement, SEP tax shifts).
  • One-stop bundles – No more coordinating separate freelancers; we handle ads (Meta/TikTok/Google), web apps/dashboards, data insights (ROAS optimization, forecasting), brand scaling, and full tax filing/compliance.
  • Results-driven approach – Weekly reports on leads, revenue lift, ROAS, and compliance status. Pilots show 20–40% average growth in traffic/sales while keeping you audit-ready.
  • Proven for Kenyan sectors – From fintech user acquisition and e-com conversion boosts to real estate lead gen and SME tax optimization.

Whether you're a growing lending app, fashion e-shop, or property developer, EliteEdge turns challenges into opportunities. Let's build your edge in Kenya's digital economy – compliant, scalable, and profitable.
